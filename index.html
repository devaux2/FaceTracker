Quick start

  1. Open the Control panel and the Display (use the button — keep both in this same browser).
  2. On the display, allow the camera and press F for full screen.
  3. In the control panel, download the paint template, paint on it, and upload a transparent PNG.

Tip: serve this folder over http://localhost (see README) so the camera and live sync work reliably.
